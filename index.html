Advancing Regional Space Capabilities

The SIKAP+ Project represents a transformative initiative to enhance regional capacity in space infrastructure, applications, and knowledge transfer through collaborative training and promotion across Asia. Leveraging the existing space assets and expertise of the Philippines and partner countries, this project provides a comprehensive platform for exchanging best practices in satellite communication, Earth observation (EO), and space situational awareness technologies.

Project Mission & Objectives

The initiative advances regional cooperation through shared use of Ground Receiving Stations (GRS), cutting-edge data processing technologies, and open-source monitoring tools. By engaging new and emerging space actors across Asia, SIKAP+ contributes significantly to the responsible and peaceful use of outer space technologies for societal benefit, environmental monitoring, and sustainable development across the region.

Space Infrastructure & Technology

SIKAP+ leverages state-of-the-art space infrastructure to create a collaborative ecosystem for space technology development and knowledge sharing. The project focuses on building regional capacity through shared access to advanced satellite systems, ground stations, and data processing capabilities.
